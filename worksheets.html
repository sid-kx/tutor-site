How We Teach

A simple, effective approach that builds skills and confidence.

  • Curriculum-Aligned

    Every pack maps to Ontario & Canadian expectations (Grades 1–8).

  • Skill-Tiered

    We adjust difficulty so practice is challenging but achievable.

  • Guided → Independent

    In-class coaching moves toward independent homework.

  • Assess & Feedback

    Short check-ins and quick feedback guide the next steps.

We use Canadian curriculum worksheets to reinforce key concepts across Math, Science, and English. In class, students complete guided practice with support from an OCT-certified teacher. For homework, we assign 1–2 sheets per week based on pace and level. We track progress and adjust pack difficulty to keep momentum.

  • Guided class practice with clear examples
  • 1–2 short, printable homework sheets
  • Progress tracking and targeted review
  • Downloadable PDFs; device-friendly viewing
